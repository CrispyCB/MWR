Who We Are

MW Rouzer, LLC is a New Jersey-based products company in the building materials industry. We serve tile distributors, plumbing distributors, architects and builders from the Mid-Atlantic to the Northeast region. MW Rouzer, LLC provides solutions for accessible living and decorative coverings from floor to ceiling — offering a full range of materials, including natural stone, porcelain, ceramic tile, mosaics, glass, metals, and floor preparation.

Our Services

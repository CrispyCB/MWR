Who We Are

MWR is a products company in the building materials industry. We serve tile distributors, plumbing distributors, architects and builders from the Mid-Atlantic to the Northeast region. MWR provides solutions for accessible living and decorative coverings from floor to ceiling — offering a full range of materials, including natural stone, porcelain, ceramic tile, mosaics, glass, metals, and floor preparation.

Our Services

With over a decade of experience in the floorcovering field, we direct our clients to the best products for their customers, both technically and aesthetically.

We offer our clients and their customers a range of flooring solutions for accessible living and interior design.
